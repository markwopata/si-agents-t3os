# Strategic Initiatives Operating Model

Strategic Initiatives (SIs) are an EquipmentShare operating model used by executive leadership to drive operational outcomes across teams.

At a high level:

- every SI has a concrete objective
- every SI is expected to have a triad of sales, operations, and analytics ownership
- one member of the triad is the accountable lead
- teams should maintain communication in the dedicated Slack channel
- supporting artifacts should live in the linked Google Drive folder
- the playbook should stay current with top blockers and KPI progress

Triad expectations:

- Sales drives momentum and pushes work forward
- Operations manages process, procedures, transactions, and execution detail
- Analytics owns scorekeeping, KPI definition, and tooling

This document is the seed for the global SI guidance page and can be refined in the application.
